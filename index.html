SubtlePatterns Bookmarklet Drag this to your bookmarks toolbar to preview backgrounds on any website

What is this?

Finding good background patterns for your website can be a pain. SubtlePatterns.com is a great website that makes this a lot easier by offering free patterns you can use. This bookmarklet lets you preview these patterns directly on your website, so you can quickly find the best one for your needs.

Tip: Use your left and right arrow keys to navigate between patterns (you can try it right now)
